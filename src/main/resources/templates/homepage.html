Vintage

The vintage element of second-hand furniture comes from its unique design features, materials, and craftsmanship. These elements often reflect the trends, tastes, and aesthetics of the era in which they were produced.

Unique

Second-hand furniture can have a unique element that sets it apart from new furniture pieces. Since second-hand furniture is often one-of-a-kind, it can be difficult to find the same piece elsewhere.

Refurbished

With a little creativity and some elbow grease, a worn-out piece of furniture can become a stunning statement piece that adds character and style to a space.

About Us


Timeless Finds offers carefully curated vintage pieces that can be incorporated seamlessly into modern-day interiors.

In 2010, Sarah opened Timeless Finds, a boutique furniture store that specializes in high-quality antique furniture and vintage wares from all over the world. Each piece has been carefully selected for its beauty, craftsmanship, and story.

Timeless Finds has become a go-to destination for designers and collectors alike. Our passion for unique, well-crafted pieces has inspired her team to curate an ever-changing collection of beautiful, one-of-a-kind furniture.
